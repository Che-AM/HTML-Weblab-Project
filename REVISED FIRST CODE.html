Nicholas Britell

Nicholas Britell is Emmy award winning and grammy nominated American composer, notable for his works with director Barry Jenkins as well as scoring the TV show succession. Born in 1980, in New York, NY he’s quickly becoming one of the most regarded names in the modern film composition landscape.

Another Fun fact

Nicholas Britell produced hip hop in college while studying composition at harvard. This influenced is clearly seen in the trap elements of his Succession score

Accolades

  • Won the 2019 Primetime Emmy Award Outstanding Main Title Theme Music (Succession)
  • Nominated for best original score at the academy awards and golden globe awards 2016 and 2018 Moonlight and Beale street
  • Nominated for the 2023 grammy for best score soundtrack for visual media (sucession)
